Ryan Li


Our Head Secretary and Secretary of Meetings and Planning, Ryan enjoys playing sports such as football and basketball with friends. He also enjoys debating with others about current issues, especially politics, frequently having clashing points of view with others, resulting in very insightful discussions. When he's not debating with his friends, you can find Ryan reading random facts about random things to cure his boredom. Ryan started doing Model UN last year in May, as a result of quarantine boredom, and it has quickly become one of his passions. Since then, he has attended 3 conferences, being PWVMUN, VYMUN, and VMUN, frequently winning awards. His highlight of his MUN career so far has been roasting his fellow exec team member, Ayaan, in question period in the House of Commons committee at VMUN this past January.


Penelope Dierkes


Penelope, along with Navya, run the PWMUNC Instagram account, posting content such as facts about MUN, meeting reminders and much more! She joined Model UN to improve her public speaking and debating skills, and thoroughly enjoys her experience. The best part of MUN is the conferences of course! Engaging in debate and compromising to pass resolution papers is a highlight along with meeting lots of new people. In her spare time, Penelope plays volleyball, piano, and enjoys baking and reading.
